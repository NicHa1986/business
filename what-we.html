Data Engineering

Some clients need us for converting data files and adapting them afterwards in a way that is more readable to them (the usual steps of data wrangling: Merging, Selecting, Filtering, Grouping). Furthermore, we also look for improvements in the storage process of data, for example in terms of data redundancy, efficiency and safety. Because different database structures have different advantages, we try to discuss these issues with our clients and try to find optimal solutions based on specific interests and wishes that our clients have.

Data Analytics

Data Analysis is all about measuring.Related to this, one can use KPI's or statistical measures. We have the experience to combine both and presenting our clients the most relevant information in easy words. Beside working with simple measures of the raw data, one can use a model that makes some assumptions and therefroe uses raw data to transform it. In Data Analytics, the modelling worlds differentiates between descriptive and prescriptive/predictive tools. The latter already has a specific target/goal included and also realted to it. The former is less structured and treates all the variables almost the same way (without thinking about a particular target). Descriptive Analytics therefore often looks for simple relationships and patterns in the data and is hence mostly a good starting point.

Business Analytics

In the financial world, the step of forecasting is much more diffcult than in natural science because Economics is about people and people deal with uncertainty. In other words, because people think differently depending on the current state of the society, their consumption and investment decisions differentiate too. However, we always try to help our clients in combining the current state of their businesses with future expectations. In using adequate KPI's and statistical measures, we try to reduce that uncertainty and present more balanced (less variant) solutions.
